Price Predictor

This web application is meant to predict the price of different homes in the Sacramento area. By giving us the homes Postal Code, Square Foot, Number of Bedrooms and Number of Washrooms of the home we can try and predict the homes current value. You can also fill in the year if you want to find the homes value from a different time
